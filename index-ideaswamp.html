2 _____ mockups

super self indulgent book/website/interactive PDF anthology collecting texts about design mockups, specifically how they relate to other formats, skeletons, structures

previously: Sincerely Yours (on the utopic potential of mockups, which are decontextualized), mockupcore: gestures of transgression (on wheatpaste textures)

reference: George Kafka, "Are Mockup Designers the Most Influential Designers of Our Era?"

general ideas: mockups as still lifes, politics of aspect ratios, other defaults: the default white cube in 3d sculpting programs

Added by leslie liu @ 09:45pm on 2021-Sep-11
